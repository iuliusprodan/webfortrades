# Build notes - Amrock Electrical Ltd (Path B)

## Hero decision: TYPOGRAPHIC (no photo)

Only 3 Google photos exist and only ONE is usable finished work:
- `01-places.webp` - WORK IN PROGRESS: a living room mid-install (cardboard box, tools on the
  floor, a person crouched). Banned as a hero per the site-design skill (no WIP / clutter / people).
- `02-places.webp` - FINISHED: a tidy completed living-room media wall with an electric fire
  installed, TV mounted. The one genuinely usable finished shot; on-angle (matches Simon's
  electric-fire review and the older-home heritage angle).
- `03-places.webp` - the brand LOGO (red swoosh + wordmark on black), not a project photo. Tiny
  (828x331). Not a hero.

Decision: a **strong typographic / solid warm-earth hero** (allowed by the skill when no finished
photo is suitable). Reasons: (a) one domestic living-room shot reads as a single room, not a
portfolio; (b) spending it on a full-bleed hero would leave the gallery empty; (c) the brand's own
red-on-black logo makes a bold typographic terracotta hero on-brand. The single good photo
(`02-places.webp`) is featured in the **selected-work** section where it does the most good.

Hero treatment: `--ink` brown-black ground + a faint terracotta-tinted brick-course texture (CSS
only, no image, no AI), a short terracotta rule, heavy Inter Tight headline. Confirmed at screenshot
verification.

## Palette + fonts
- Family: warm-earth. Accent: fired terracotta/rust `#A8492A` (deep `#8C3A20`). Ink `#241D19`.
  Stone bg `#E4E0DA` (cool clay, NOT cream). Mid `#D8D2C8`. Line `#C6BEB2`. Muted `#6E6457`.
- Body: Inter. Display: Inter Tight (sans-humanist), heavy/large.
- Distinct from Damo (golden cedar-amber + sawn-timber): Amrock is redder/earthier on a cooler stone.
  Not the warm-cream + serif + terracotta cluster (cool greyed stone + sans display + true brick red).

## Signature motif
Double "brick-course" rule dividers (4px double `--line`) between sections - a mortar joint between
brick courses, echoing the older-home brickwork the trade works on.
