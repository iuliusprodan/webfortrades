# Email touch 1

One email. Site link on its own line with blank lines above and below.

Subject: `One-page site for {business_name}`

Redesign / HAS_REAL_SITE subject example: `Alternative direction for {area} {trade_plural}`

```
Hi {owner_first_name_or_there},

I'm Julius.

I built a one-page site for {business_name} here:

{site_url}

I used the public details I could find and kept it simple: services, reviews, areas covered, and a quote form.

If it is not useful, reply no thanks and I will take it down.

Julius
```

Redesign opening variant (replace the "I built" paragraph):

```
We put together an alternative direction for {area} {trade_plural} and thought I'd send it over in case it's useful.
```

Rules: blank lines around every URL. No attachment references.
