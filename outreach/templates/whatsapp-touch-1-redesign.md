# WhatsApp touch 1 (redesign / HAS_REAL_SITE)

Use when the business already has a working website. Acknowledge an alternative direction without criticising their current site.

One message. Site link only, with blank lines above and below the URL.

```
Hi, is this {business_name}?

I'm Julius. We put together an alternative direction for {area} {trade_plural} and thought I'd send it over in case it's useful.

{site_url}

Happy to change anything on it. If you'd like to keep it, just let me know.
```

Placeholders:
- `{area}` - town or region (e.g. Sheffield)
- `{trade_plural}` - trade in plural form (e.g. roofers, plumbers)

Rules: blank lines around every URL. No critique of the existing site.

If video attachments are later enabled, see `legacy/legacy-video-shape.md`.
