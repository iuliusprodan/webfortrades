## Design direction (id: swansea_atelier_deep_green_brass_editorial)

No logo exists, so the palette comes from the photos and the name.

- Deep heritage green `#21402E` for the hero canvas, footer and headings. Motivated by the name Greens and a calm, trustworthy tone.
- Brass and antique gold `#B08A4F` for accents, call-to-action buttons and hairline rules. Taken from the real brass bath and shower fittings in the photos.
- Warm stone `#CBBEA8` for section bands. From the marble and tiled walls.
- Ivory `#F7F3EC` page background and `#FFFFFF` cards. From the marble and walls.
- Ink `#15201A` for body text.

Fonts: Cormorant Garamond for display, Outfit for body. An elegant high-contrast serif with a clean modern grotesque suits premium bathroom work, and is distinct from every recent WebForTrades site.

Layout: editorial and immersive. A deep green hero with an asymmetric split, the wordmark, headline, proof line and two brass buttons on one side, the signature bathroom photo on the other. Brass hairline rules. Alternate warm stone and ivory section bands rather than one repeating pattern. A magazine-style varied gallery grid, not uniform squares. One large pulled review quote on a stone band. Generous whitespace and refined serif headings.

Calls to action: Call 07309 553552 and Email info@gpplumbingltd.com. Brass primary button, outlined secondary. A slim sticky call bar on mobile.

Animation: subtle fade and slide-in on scroll, smooth anchor scrolling, a gentle image hover zoom and clear button hover states. Respect prefers-reduced-motion.

Deliberately different from: Corvell (warm cream and terracotta bathroom, Source Serif and Manrope), and the earlier Greens template idea (forest green accent on cream, Fraunces and Inter, stacked hero). Avoid the headline "done properly". Avoid generic plumber blue and red.
