# Creative brief: Greens Precise Plumbing & Heating Ltd

This brief follows the site strategy and section plan, and feeds the Open Design pilot. It is a design-only concept. No outreach, no deploy.

## Logo and colour decision (colour rule)

- Logo available: no. Logo discovery ran and failed. The email-domain site gpplumbingltd.com returns a 202 bot or access block, and the verified Facebook page assets contained no usable logo.
- Because there is no logo, the palette is derived from the verified photos and the business name, in line with the rule: prefer the logo, otherwise photos, then trade and name.

Palette:

- Deep heritage green `#21402E`. From the business name Greens and a calm, trustworthy trade tone.
- Brass and antique gold `#B08A4F`. From the real brass bath and shower fittings visible in the photos.
- Warm stone `#CBBEA8`. From the marble and tiled walls in the bathroom photos.
- Ivory `#F7F3EC` and white `#FFFFFF`. From the marble and bright walls.
- Ink `#15201A`. For legible body text.

## Fonts

- Display: Cormorant Garamond. Body: Outfit.
- An elegant high-contrast serif with a clean modern grotesque suits premium bathroom work, and is distinct from Fraunces, Inter, Source Serif, Manrope, Space Grotesk, Archivo and Lora used across recent sites.

## Layout direction

Editorial and immersive. Deep green asymmetric hero, the signature bathroom photo alongside the wordmark, headline, proof line and two brass buttons. Brass hairline rules. Warm stone and ivory section bands. A magazine-style varied gallery rather than uniform squares. One large pulled review quote on a stone band.

## Tone of voice

Assured, precise, premium and friendly. Plain British English. No hype, no jargon, no em dashes.

## Anti-clone notes

- Corvell already used a warm cream and terracotta premium bathroom direction. This must look clearly different, so Greens uses a deep green and brass editorial treatment.
- The earlier Greens template idea was forest green accent on cream with Fraunces and Inter and a stacked hero. This pilot diverges with an immersive deep green canvas, brass and marble, Cormorant Garamond and Outfit, and an asymmetric hero.

## Evidence anchors

- 5.0 on Google across 45 reviews, 100% recommend on Facebook from 10 reviews.
- Named team member Callum, from reviews, used as a team name only.
- Real bathroom and kitchen renovation photos, brass fittings, marble and warm stone.
- Based in Swansea, recent kitchen work in The Mumbles. Never Bristol.
